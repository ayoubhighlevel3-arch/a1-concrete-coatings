WHO WE ARE

The Commercial Flooring Specialists

A1 Concrete Coatings is built exclusively for commercial and industrial clients who need floors that perform as hard as their operations.

We specialize in high-performance polyurea and polyaspartic concrete coating systems designed for the demands of industry — not residential touch-ups. How It Works

Mission-ready results in three simple steps.

1

Prep & Repair

We use industrial diamond grinders to mechanically abrade the concrete and repair all cracks and pits, ensuring a perfect bond.
2

Base & Flake

A 100% solids base coat is applied, followed by a full broadcast of decorative vinyl flakes for maximum durability and style.
3

Seal & Protect

Our UV-stable Polyaspartic topcoat locks it all in, providing superior chemical resistance and a showroom shine. Ready in 24 hours.
