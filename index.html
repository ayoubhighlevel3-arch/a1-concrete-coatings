WHO WE ARE

Two Flooring Systems. Built for Business.

A1 Concrete Coatings brings two elite flooring systems to commercial facilities across West Michigan — high-performance polyurea & flake coatings built for durability, and premium polished concrete built for lasting beauty.

Whether your facility needs a chemical-resistant polyurea coating with a full vinyl flake broadcast, or a mirror-finish diamond-polished concrete surface, we have the crew, the equipment, and the expertise to deliver it right. Our polyurea and polyaspartic systems bond at a molecular level and cure in 24 hours. Our polished concrete process follows a precise diamond-grit progression — producing surfaces that densify, harden, and hold their finish for decades. Two systems. One expert team. No downtime.

How It Works

Same disciplined process, whether it's coatings or polished concrete.

1

Site Assessment

We visit your facility, evaluate the concrete condition, and recommend the right system — coatings or polish — at no cost and no obligation.
2

Surface Prep

Industrial diamond grinders mechanically prepare every surface, repair cracks and pits, and create the perfect foundation for either system.
3

Install & Finish

Coating systems are applied and back in service within 24 hours. Polished concrete is diamond-refined to your chosen sheen — satin to mirror.
