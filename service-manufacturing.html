Engineered for Heavy Machinery & High Impact

Manufacturing plants and machine shops subject floors to brutal abuse. From heavy machinery vibrations and dropped parts to solvent spills and steel-wheeled carts. A1 Concrete Coatings installs highly engineered, impact-resistant systems designed to protect the concrete slab, prevent concrete degradation, and provide a safe walking surface.

  • Heavy Machinery Load Rated
  • Chemical & Solvent Resistant

  • Thermal Shock Resistant Capabilities

Why Choose Our Manufacturing System?

Because you need a floor that works as hard as your facility does.
