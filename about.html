OUR STORY

Built in Grand Rapids.
Built for Industry.

A1 Concrete Coatings was founded with one mission: to give commercial and industrial clients in West Michigan a contractor they could actually trust. We saw too many operations stuck with peeling epoxy floors, blown-out budgets, and crews that disappeared mid-job.

We built the company differently. Industrial-grade diamond grinders. Commercial-spec polyurea and polyaspartic systems. Crews trained to work around your operation — nights, weekends, phased installations — so you never lose a day of production.

Today, we are West Michigan's go-to commercial floor coating specialist.
