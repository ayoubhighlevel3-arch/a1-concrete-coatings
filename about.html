Our Story

We Built This Company
Because the Market Failed You.

A1 Concrete Coatings was founded with one mission: to give commercial and industrial clients in West Michigan a contractor they could actually trust. We watched too many facility managers get burned — peeling epoxy floors after 18 months, blown-out budgets, crews that vanished mid-job, and cut-rate materials dressed up in slick sales pitches.

We built the company differently. We invested in industrial planetary diamond grinders instead of acid etching. We spec commercial-grade polyurea and polyaspartic systems instead of consumer epoxy kits. We hire and train crews specifically to work around your operation — nights, weekends, phased installations — because your facility can't afford to lose a day of production for a floor.

We don't do residential. We don't dabble in driveways. Every project we take on is a commercial or industrial facility that demands a floor coating that performs as hard as the operation running on top of it.

Today, we are West Michigan's go-to commercial floor coating specialist.

Commercial-Only Contractor

The Principles We Operate By

Craftsmanship Over Speed

Every floor goes through the same rigorous 6-phase process. We don't rush prep to make a deadline. Good enough doesn't exist in our vocabulary — especially not on an industrial floor that has to survive forklift traffic for 15 years.

Straight Talk, No Surprises

We tell you what the floor needs, what the system will cost, and what you can realistically expect. We'd rather lose a job on honesty than win it on a promise we can't keep.

Commercial-Grade or Nothing

We use the same materials on a 500 sq ft auto shop as we do on a 50,000 sq ft distribution warehouse. Industrial polyurea base, UV-stable polyaspartic topcoat — every time, no exceptions.
