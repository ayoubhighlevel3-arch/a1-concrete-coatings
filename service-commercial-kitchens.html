HEALTH & SAFETY

Safety and Sanitation Built In

Commercial kitchens are a notoriously harsh environment for flooring. Hot oil spills, heavy foot traffic, and constant wet conditions pose a serious slip hazard and sanitation risk. Our coating systems are highly textured for maximum grip, impervious to grease, and fully seamless to satisfy the most demanding health department inspections.

  • Grease & Oil Resistant Surface
  • Anti-Slip Texture for Wet Conditions
  • Antibacterial & Antimicrobial Coating
  • Easy to Sanitize & Deep Clean
  • Withstands High-Temp Steam Cleaning
  • Health Department Compliant

Why Choose Our Kitchen System?

Designed to survive the constant abuse of a professional kitchen environment.

Hot Oil Resistance

Protects the concrete substrate from degradation caused by hot cooking oil spills and fat drippings.

Superior Traction

Aggressive quartz or aluminum oxide textured finishes provide sure-footing for staff in constantly wet and greasy conditions.

Waterproof Flashing

Integral cove base applications seamlessly connect the floor to the wall, creating a watertight basin that prevents leaks and hidden bacterial growth.
